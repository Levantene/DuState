Should You Invest in Real Estate in Dubai?

Since 2020 prices for residential real estate in Dubai have been showing a steady increase and nearly doubled from that time.

It’s a well-known fact that residential real estate market in Dubai is cyclical and follows a global economic trend.

For many people investing in real estate in Dubai can be a lucrative opportunity. But is it still a great investment? To keep it simple – let’s dive into a very simple rule: the law of supply and demand.

Demand Side

Dubai’s population keeps increasing. More people need more shelters and that mostly drives a demand for real estate.

Dubai became a home for many people as it emerged to be economic, business and tourism hub. That city offers a rapid opportunity that is being attracted by more people.

But will that trend continue? A simple answer – Yes.

More and more people keep coming to Dubai and its population is expected to reach 5.8 million by 2040 (vs nearly 3.7 million as of today) – 50% increase.

That is a unique feature of Dubia given the fact the population in most of the parts of the world is shrinking (China, Japan, Europe to say as an example).

Supply Side

It’s not a secret that Dubai has been experiencing influx of new residential projects. Well known developers like Emaar, Nakheel, DAMAC continue to launch new projects that never end.

To make it straight – oversupply may be a clear concern that could lead to price correction. But when it would happen given cyclicality feature of Dubai residential sector?

Nobody could predict a future.

It all comes down to careful selection of residential projects.

Location, project, property type together with extensive market research – all of these should be taken into a serious consideration.

At the same time, defining short-term/long-term plans could also protect from downside risk.

Set your investment goal with a right horizon and financing terms before making an investment. That would define a success of investment into real estate sector in Dubai.

There is no double that Dubai continues its pace of being rapid development center that attracts more and more people.

Like other investment classes all comes to returns and risks. And real estate market in Dubai is no exception. Well-informed strategy with a right selection of real estate project would offer substantial returns with downside protection.
